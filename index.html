Welcome

Today you and your family are one step closer toward a positive path of growth and well-being.

While we can’t change difficult situations that may have occurred in the past, we can work together to better understand and resolve challenges in your life. Following current best practices, we will unearth long-standing behavior patterns or negative perceptions that may be holding you or your family back from experiencing a more fulfilling and meaningful life.

If you’re looking for extra support and guidance through a challenging situation or you’re just ready to move in a new direction in your life, we look forward to working with you and your family to achieve your goals.

Counseling and Advocacy Associates provides supportive counseling and intervention for children, adults, and families. CAA’s community-based services promote a more comfortable and natural setting where changes are more likely to be long-lasting. Our highly personalized approach is tailored to each client’s individual needs to help them attain their desired personal growth. Our diverse and experienced staff specialize in:

  • Anxiety Disorder
  • Depression
  • Oppositional Defiant Disorder (ODD)
  • Conduct Disorder
  • Bipolar Disorder
  • Substance Abuse Disorder
  • Attention Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADD/ADHD)
  • Autism Spectrum Disorder
  • Developmental Delay
  • Intellectual Disability
